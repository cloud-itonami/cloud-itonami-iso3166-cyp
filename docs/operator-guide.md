# Operator Guide — CYP

Portal: e-PPS (eProcurement, eprocurement.gov.cy), governed by the
Public Procurement Directorate (PPD) of the Treasury of the Republic of
Cyprus under Ν. 73(Ι)/2016 (transposing EU Directive 2014/24/EU).
Entity: DRCOR incorporation (Companies Law, Cap. 113) + Τμήμα
Φορολογίας (Tax Department) record via the Φορολογική Πύλη (Tax Portal,
TAXISnet-credentialed). Engagements relying on Article 57(6)
self-cleaning (declared an exclusion ground under Article 57(1)/(4) but
bidding anyway) also clear the Code's own cumulative evidence gate
before a filing can proceed. Human-gated filing only.

1. Confirm DRCOR incorporation and Tax Department registration.
2. Intake against e-PPS (eprocurement.gov.cy), the Republic's
   e-tendering portal.
3. If the engagement declares a self-cleaning claim, confirm all three
   Article 57(6) conditions and that no final exclusion decision bars
   it.
4. Human-gated filing only -- `:filing/draft`/`:filing/submit` always
   require approval, at every rollout phase.
